How are we different?

We pledge to make Impact a lot more different from other organizations and platforms by conducting fun and innovative training sessions, as well as host our own collaborative projects with other organizations and hold webinars and interactive seminars with several motivational speakers. You may definitely ask "Why should we join Impact and how does it benefit us?" Well, keeping your best interests in mind we have lined up a range of invigorating events and competitions along with a lot more enjoyable activities tailored just for you. Also, showing deep concern about the lockdown situation, we figured out that why not keep learning and entertainment together in sync and do something productive instead of sitting idly at home, until the future arrives.
